The GDP Certification Process

1. Preparation and Paperwork

  • Create thorough documentation for all distribution operations.
  • Ensure adherence to documented procedures.

2. Implementation of GDP Recommendations

  • Integrate GDP regulations into daily distribution activities.
  • Ensure safe transit and handling practices.
  • Maintain appropriate storage settings to avoid contamination, damage, or theft.

3. Internal Audit

  • Verify all procedures adhere to GDP regulations.
  • Conduct an internal audit to confirm compliance.

4. Application Submission

  • Submit an application to the regulatory agency for GDP certification.
  • Include all required GDP compliance documentation.

5. Inspection

  • Confirm distribution procedures meet requirements.
  • The regulatory agency performs site inspections and reviews recorded processes.

6. Certification

  • Certification is granted if all standards are satisfied.
  • Re-certification is required after a certain period.
