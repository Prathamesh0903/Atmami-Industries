Our Process

GDP Certification Process

Our systematic approach ensures a smooth and efficient certification process tailored to your organization's needs.

01

Preparation & Documentation

Create comprehensive documentation for all distribution operations and ensure adherence to documented procedures.

  • Documentation review and creation
  • Procedure standardization
  • Compliance assessment
02

Implementation

Integrate GDP regulations into daily distribution activities with proper training and monitoring.

  • GDP regulation integration
  • Safe transit practices
  • Storage optimization
03

Internal Audit

Conduct thorough internal audits to verify compliance with GDP regulations and identify areas for improvement.

  • Compliance verification
  • Gap analysis
  • Corrective actions
04

Application Submission

Submit comprehensive application with all required documentation to regulatory authorities.

  • Application preparation
  • Documentation compilation
  • Regulatory submission

  • Site inspection preparation
  • Process demonstration
  • Documentation review
06

Receive official certification upon successful completion of all requirements and standards.

  • Certification issuance
  • Continuous monitoring
  • Recertification planning
